Welcome to the YZ ETH UI Demo Gallery!

This collection showcases four different design approaches for the YZ ETH blockchain interface. Each demo maintains the brown and white color scheme while exploring different layout strategies, user experience patterns, and responsive design approaches. These demos are designed to help our engineering team evaluate and choose the best direction for the production interface.
